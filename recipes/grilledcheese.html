Grilled Cheese

A crispy and cheese sandwich you can make in minutes!

This is a recipe I perfected in college. Its very fast and easy.

Once you get it down no one will be able to resist its crispy cheesy goodness.

All you need is a stove a pan and a few basic ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • American Cheese
  • Butter
  • White Bread

Steps:

  1. Heat a generous amount of butter in a pan. (very generous)
  2. Once the butter in hot and bubbly toast two bread slices until brown on each side.
  3. Decide which side of each bread slice has the best browning.
  4. Face one of the slices with the best brown side up in the still heated pan.
  5. Unwrap and place two peices of cheese on the slice of bread in the pan.
  6. Place the other slce of bread on the cheese with the best crisped side facing the cheese.
  7. Cook the sandwich on both sides until the bread is crisped and the cheese is runny
  8. (Optional) lightly salt and pepper.
